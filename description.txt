[h1]Hello, Doctor![/h1]
[h1]-------------------------------------------------------------------[/h1]
This is a fan-made Terraria mod for Doctors who want to play more Arknights while already playing Arknights.
Perfect for farming or waiting through maintenance. Please enjoy to your heart’s content a fun and relaxing vacation in Terraria.
[h1]-------------------------------------------------------------------[/h1]
[h1]What is Arknights?[/h1]
Arknights is a tower defense (TD) game for smartphones. (Developed by: Hypergryph / Published by: Yostar)
If you’re a Doctor suffering from memory loss and can’t quite remember even after this explanation, please check below:
[url=https://www.arknights.global]Official Arknights Website[/url]
If you’re a Doctor who loves Arknights, you’ll surely enjoy this mod as well.
[h1]-------------------------------------------------------------------[/h1]
[h1]What can you do in this mod?[/h1]
As a Doctor who awakens in the world of Terraria, you can fight against familiar-looking enemies while using weapons and gear inspired by various Operators.
